The New Values

by A:TNG - Mar 10th, 2014 - posted in values

A:TNG adds four values Agile needs at enterprise scale over the original manifesto:

  • Pattern languages over prescriptive frameworks
  • Productive teams over certified individuals
  • Metric feedback over command & control
  • Consensus protocols over management hierarchies

That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.

Why What How

by A:TNG - Mar 9th, 2014 -

Why is it that the Agile Manifesto hasn’t changed since 2001? There are conferences every year. Have we learned nothing in all that time? Seeing the Manifesto as some historical document like the Declaration of Independence doesn’t seem very Agile. Don’t we embrace change around here?

The stand-up in the Med that presaged the famous Snowbird meeting was where Extreme really became Agile. That was the beauty of it. Nothing was sacred and there was no need to pay our respect to any great names. We were conspirators and it was a revolution.
