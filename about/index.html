About

Mar 9th, 2014

A:TNG is an initiative to re-open the community of Agile practitioners that originally fostered XP to focus on the new frontier of Agile at enterprise scale.

A:TNG identifies the state of the Agile art through patterns contributed by its community of Agilists in the trenches. It will distribute this work via github under an open content license to enable the community to curate and refactor these patterns to form an Open Agile Pattern Language.

Conspiracy

Robert Heinlein said that inside every revolution there’s a conspiracy. We aim to open the gates to all comers now so it won’t matter who kicked this thing off. If we do that right, A:TNG will evolve over time in ways we can’t possibly anticipate. We hope you’ll contribute ideas and feedback to take part in this revolution yourself.

  • Peter Merel — Tea Master & Editor in Chief

  • Dave Geving — Chargé D’Affaires Américains

  • Dave Martin — Master at Arms and Brewing

  • Dave Bales — Lean Waterfowl, Certified Scrum Everything

  • The Masked Methodologist — Architecture & Sports Desk

If you’re sympathetic, fork us on github. We have a lot of work to do just on the basics before we get the collaborative pattern authoring flow going, but forking will helps us feel we’re not plumb loco here. And it’s good for the soul.
